Kids Program
   Our program is a great way to improve discipline and self-esteem in kids. Our classes include specific age groups, however, we encourage younger students to interact with older peers once they reach a certain level of technique in order to make them comfortable with different sized opponents.
   We believe that the best anti-bullying program is not focused on the “bullies” themselves, but instead creates a positive environment where self-defense and assertiveness becomes second nature. The kids are not instigated to fight, they are encouraged to be respectful, kind, take turns, and when needed, use body control and submissions to defend themselves.
   There are no punch exchanges in our classes. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu focuses on using the opponent’s own body weight and power against themselves.
   Carlson Gracie academies uses the IBJJF belt system program.
Adult Program
   These classes are available throughout the week, two classes in the morning and one in the evening, so you can easily fit jiu jitsu training into your schedule.
   The student will learn techniques and principles that made Brazilian jiu-jitsu the most efficient and effective fighting art in the world.
    We carry on Carlson Gracie Sr's legacy of authentic style BJJ, family, tough love, kindness, and humility.
Competition Team
   Our students compete at the highest level tournaments and hold numerous titles at the World, Pan American and Open Championships at IBJJF, NABJJF, JJWL, Abu Dhabi, BJJ PRO and NAGA federations. We also have students competing at UFC and EBI tournaments.
